About Me

First of all, hello, my name is Matt. I'm a Computer Science student at Edge Hill University as well as an aspiring software engineer. The purpose of this website, that I have created, is to show off my previous work, things that I am working on now as well as talk a little bit more about me.

My Achievements So Far

So far, whilst undertaking my degree, I have been expanding my knowledge by completing various other courses online as well as working on my own projects on the side, some of which you can find on this website and on my GitHub. In addition to this, I considered myself a valuable member of the computer science departments Research Club where as a group we completed various projects, some of which were published. I was also responsible for setting up and being president of the universitys Coding Club Society in which I ran the club and gave other programmers a space to work together outside of lectures and seminars on projects or general enhancing of their coding ability.
